Together Pottsville

togetherpottsville@gmail.com

Resilient Pottsville initiative:

  • Workshop: Designing a better future together: Imagine Northern Rivers and Flood to Flourish is running a workshop for what a regenerative future looks like for Tweed Shire. Date/time/location: Tue Apr 21 from 10am-12:30pm in Murwillumbah. We encourage other groups to join so we can work together to create a more resilient, regenerative future for Pottsville. You get a gift card for attending. Sign up at: https://events.humanitix.com/imagine-nr-floodtoflourish-murwillumbah 
  • Emergency communications plan: We’re putting together a plan for our resilience network to communicate when there is no telco/power available in town. We’re seeking advice from anyone with radio experience, if that is you please reach out.

Pottsville Beach Public School P&C Association

pandc.pottsville@gmail.com

We are Pottsville Beach Public School P&C Association and we connect with families, strengthen our school community, and support important school initiatives through fundraising and volunteering.

  • Our Eco Warrior Garden Club is needing donations and skills! You can help our kids by donating materials such as PVC pipe with holes for planting and drainage, soil, fertilizer or time!
  • Return and Earn: We have also officially registered as a charity with the Return and Earn program! To donate your container refunds directly to our school; Open the Return and Earn App and find the Charities tab and search/select Pottsville Beach Public School P&C and set as payout.
  • Donations: Every donation goes towards helping our kids thrive with the resources they deserve. If you wish to lend us your support, or have some fundraising opportunities, please send us an email to pandc.pottsville@gmail.com
