Pottsville Updates

Welcome to the latest issue of the Pottsville Joint Newsletter.

This bi-monthly update shares news and activities from community groups and organisations across Pottsville. If you would like to be featured in the next issue, please email us at togetherpottsville@gmail.com.

Together Pottsville

togetherpottsville@gmail.com

  • Designing a better future together workshop share-back: Imagine Northern Rivers and Flood to Flourish hosted a workshop back in April to envision a regenerative future for the Tweed Shire. They are offering to share their findings to interested Pottsville groups/residents in the near future. We invite all local groups and residents to attend this session to help shape our collective future. If you are interested, please email us and we’ll organise a session.
  • Seeking radio operators: We’re putting together a plan for our resilience network to communicate when there is no telco/power available in town. We’re seeking anyone with radio experience who can provide advice and help us do some testing.
  • Bag swap stations: We're giving the bag swap stations at IGA and Foodworks a glow-up with brand-new, clearer signs. Stay tuned for the big reveal!

info@dolphinresearchaustralia.org

  • Caldera Environment Festival: We will have our Ocean conservation stall at the festival in Murwillumbah on 7th June
  • Byron-Tweed Hope Spot Ocean Champion Program: We have launched our Byron-Tweed Hope Spot Ocean Champion Program with great opportunities for individuals, community groups and schools to get involved in grass-roots oceans conservation actions. More information HERE (business incentives to follow)
  • Upcoming events in coming months (dates TBC):  marine debris surveys, water quality & seagrass surveys in Mooball Creek (& Cudgen + Cudgera Creeks) as part of our Byron-Tweed Hope Spot Ocean Champion Program.

  • Volunteer hub: We also have an environment & citizen science volunteer hub set up on our website for anyone interested HERE – this list will continually be updated and grow

Pottsville Beach Public School P&C Association

pandc.pottsville@gmail.com 

We are Pottsville Beach Public School P&C Association. We connect with families, strengthen our school community, and support important school initiatives through fundraising and volunteering.

  • Bunnings Sausage Sizzle: The P&C have organised a Bunnings Sausage Sizzle for Sunday, 2 August! Come help raise funds for our school! We will be there from 8am to 4pm at Bunnings in Tweed. We would love you to come down and support our School!
  • Thank you Pottsville Bowls Club: We also want to give a shout out to the team at Pottsville Bowls Club for their ongoing support, especially with our Swim Intensive Program, School Choir and Band. Your generosity is deeply appreciated by our entire school community and helps us provide these wonderful opportunities for our students.
  • Tools needed: Our Eco Warrior Garden Club is still in need of donations! We are in need of a shovel, and hand tools in good condition. If anyone can donate these items, please email us to arrange a collection.

Pottsville Community Association

secretary@pottsvillecommunityassociation.org

  • Oval fence damage: After the damage to the Pottsville Oval Fence the Tweed Shire Council moved the mobile CCTV from Banora to between the tennis courts and the oval. This is for a six month trial period, however we are hoping that the trial period will be extended.
  • Report issues to police: Residents are reminded to report all unruly behaviour, graffiti and e-bike problems to the police – 131 444. Only reports made directly to Police are counted, recorded and acted upon.
  • Current projects: We are working to get Fairy Lights in the trees, Artistic painting of power poles and Energex boxes, and getting more CCTV cameras along Coronation Avenue, Philip Street and Elizabeth Street.
  • PCA Monthly Meetings:  last Tuesday of the month – 6.30 pm at St Marks Anglican Church. Join and get meeting minutes emailed to you. Ph: 0407 889 168 or email us.
